Cookies Policy

Effective Date: 24/10/2025

The Fintalk uses cookies and other tracking tools to increase users' browsing experience and provide them with a personalized service. This cookies policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and user rights to control their use.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies help to store information and improve your browsing experience whenever you click to the website. They give detailed information about your visit, which enables the website to remember you.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Very important for the website to execute core activities properly (e.g., form submission).
  • Analytical / Performance Cookies – Collect pseudonymous data about how visitors use the website (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies – Remember preferences like language or location to provide a more customized experience.
  • Advertising / Targeting Cookies – Deliver relevant ads based on browsing behavior; may be set by us or third-party partners.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • To understand visitors' behavior.
  • To improve website performance and functionality.
  • To customize content and advertisements, and to help with security and fraud prevention.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services may place cookies on our website for social media integration, analytics, or advertising. These include:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook Pixel
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag

5. Your Choices

You can manage or delete cookies using your browser settings at any time. Options typically include:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete specific cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

6. Updates to This Policy

We may periodically update this cookies policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
